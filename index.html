The HTW-Party written by: Luis 23rd October 2017

This weekend, from 21st October at 10pm until 22nd October at 7am the HTW Party took place in the Kino International at Alexanderplatz. It is one of the biggest student partys in Berlin. This year there were four floors: There was a floor for 90s, Trash, Pop and Party; a floor for Charts, House, Black, Electro and Latin; a floor for Rock and Indie; and a floor for Deep House, Tech House and Techno.
