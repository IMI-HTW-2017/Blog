Bernhard

I love weird and interesting sounds and good sound systems. I spend my money on concerts and music. Luis

Hey, I'm Luis! I am one of the authors of this blog and I love music, but you probably guessed that already ;) I mostly listen to EDM genres like Tropical House or Future Bass, but there are many other genres I enjoy listening to as well, especially stuff from the 80s, e.g. Queen. I'm not what you would call a "Partylöwe" in German, but if there are any special events I wouldn't say no to going there either (like you can see, or rather read, in our article about the HTW party). If you happen to meet me on the streets you will probably see me wearing my Beats headphones, because I like to listen to music not only at home, but also while I'm wandering around.
