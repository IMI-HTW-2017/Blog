An Interesting Fact for Christmas Time written by: Luis 10th December 2017

Today I'll tell you an interesting fact about the YouTube stats and christmas time. Each year, when people start feeling christmassy, many also start listening to christmas songs again. And, of course, many also start listening to the all-time-classic Last Christmas by Wham!

While many people are using streaming services like Spotify or Apple Music, there are still some who use YouTube to listen to their favourite songs. Before the redesign of YouTube, there was a feature called Statistics, which you could access for each video where it wasn't deactivated. The statistics used to show a graph with a timeline going back some years and the views at a specific time mapped to it.

Because most people don't listen to Christmas songs when it's not Christmas time, the views of the Last Christmas music video are pretty low over the year. But each year in November, when the first people start listening to Christmas songs again, the graph started to rise again. So the YouTube statistics basically showed that Christmas time was starting again!
